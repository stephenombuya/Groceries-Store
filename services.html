Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers to common queries about our store and services below.

What are your store hours?

We are open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Monday to Saturday, and from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM on Sundays.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we provide home delivery services within our local area. Additional fees may apply.

Are your products sourced locally?

We prioritize sourcing locally to support our community, while also offering select international products for variety.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes, we offer a loyalty program to reward our valued customers with exclusive benefits and discounts.

Can I place an order online?

Absolutely! You can easily place your grocery orders online through our user-friendly website.
